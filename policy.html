Our Regulations

Rules

1. We only order food on Monday, Tuesday, and Friday. Each day has its own restaurant.

2. You must order one day prior, before 11:59 pm.

3. You must prepare the money beforehand on the next day. Please directly give us the money before 11:00 am. Find the officers in 12B or 12A.

4. You can preorder for the other days in each week as well. For example, if it's Sunday right now and you want to order for both Monday and Friday, then go ahead. Give us the money separately.

5. The benefits we provide are stated in the "About" page.

Ordering Procedure

1. Click on the "Order Now" button.

2. You will be transferred to a google survey.

3. Input your name and class/office, email, day, and desired meal.

4. Submit the form.

5. We will deliver the food before 12:20 pm the next day.
